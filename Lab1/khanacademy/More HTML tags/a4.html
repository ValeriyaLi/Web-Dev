Recipe #2

Steak

  • Serves: 15 min
Ingredients Quantity
Meat(Steak) 1
Salt 1 tea spoon
Soy sauce 1 table spoon

Step 1: Marinate meat with soy sauce and salt for 20 minutes.

Step 2: Heat up a skillet with oil.

Step 3: Fry the meat on each side for 15 minutes.
